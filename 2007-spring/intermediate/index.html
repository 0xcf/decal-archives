Additional Info

  • Who should take this course?
    Some familiarity with the UNIX shell is recommended. Students with with little or no prior UNIX experience are welcome, though they may have difficulty with the pace in the beginning of the course. It is our goal that at the end of this course, you will be comfortable and familiar with the command line interface in order to use and administer Unix operating systems as well as setting up a basic web server.
  • How much time should I expect to spend on this DeCal?
    The workload for the intermediate section should not be hard and I expect that it will not consume too much of your time. Aside from attending lecture, working on the labs and homework should take around an hour at most and work, in general, will ease up during midterms as facilitators are students, too. It is entirely up to you as to how much you want to spend working on the projects and labs; there are always opportunities to expand upon what has been taught and the easiest way to learn Unix system administration is through doing, not just reading.
