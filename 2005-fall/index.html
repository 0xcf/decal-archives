    Course Syllabus (tentative)

    Week 1: Introduction and History of Unix
    Week 2: Unix Commands and Text Editor(VIM)
    Week 3: DNS
    Week 4: Apache, ftpd, sshd, and other daemons; filesystems

    Week 7: Basic Programming and Perl
    Week 8: Perl (Part II)
