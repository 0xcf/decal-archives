Part I: Software List

Your group will be given a list of software to install. It is your group's responsibility to install all these programs in your OCF account. Some things you should keep in mind:
  • Your webspace (public_html) quota is 120 MB
  • Your shell (home) quota is 200 MB
If you run into disk space qoutas, you can (possibly) work through your group member's accounts as well. If you encounter this problem, contact us as a group and we'll work something out.

Part II: Virtual Hosts

Your group also has to have at least two domain names registered to your webserver. This is to support virtual hosting. Your webserver will have to serve different content depending on how your website is accessed, of course.
